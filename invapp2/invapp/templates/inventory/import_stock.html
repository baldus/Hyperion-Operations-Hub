Import Stock Adjustments

After the upload you'll assign each CSV column to the appropriate adjustment field before the import is processed.

Expected CSV columns include: item_id, sku, location_id, location_code, quantity, lot_number, batch_id, person, reference, received_date, expiration_date, supplier_name, supplier_code, purchase_order, and notes. The location_code field may be left blank to use the unassigned staging location (UNASSIGNED).
