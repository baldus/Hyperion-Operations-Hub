Import Items

After uploading your file you'll be prompted to map each CSV column to the corresponding item field before the import is completed.

Columns that can be imported include: item_id, sku, name, type, unit, description, min_stock, notes, list_price, last_unit_cost, item_class, default_location_id, default_location_code, secondary_location_id, secondary_location_code, point_of_use_location_id, and point_of_use_location_code. Columns may be omitted if they are not needed.
